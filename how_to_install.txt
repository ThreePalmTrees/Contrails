How to install Contrails
========================

1. Drag "contrails.app" to your Applications folder.

2. Open Terminal and run this command:

xattr -cr /Applications/contrails.app

3. Launch Contrails normally from Applications.


Why is step 2 needed?
---------------------
macOS blocks apps that aren't notarized by Apple. Notarization requires
an Apple Developer Program membership ($99/year) — Apple effectively
charges indie developers a yearly toll just to let users open their
software. Until we bite that bullet, "xattr -cr" is the workaround.
